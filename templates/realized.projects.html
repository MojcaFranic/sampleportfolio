LIVING BETWEEN PARKS

Ljubljana, Slovenia

author + co-author: Davor Jerman

Renovation of middle 20th century industrial complex in residential neighborhood in southern part of Ljubljana was designed and ??? as "co-living community" project with clear intentions to offer affordable, but also meaningful? and ??? living through the years.

The project focused on two existing parks, which we attempted to bind with several connections through our building: physically with two passage and additionally with middle object made of glass, which represent also our main entrance, bar and co-working spaces.

Distinctive characteristic of our design of each housing unit: living room and kitchen ara located in ground floor and have both east and west terraces, which simulate the flow through our building. In upper levels there are sleeping quarters with very flexible ground plan, which allow us to adjust to current spatial needs.

Renovation works are kept to minimum: we keep the concrete (skeletna?)construction together with vertical communications, we add new facade cladding in energetical efficient standard.

design: Mojca Franic
